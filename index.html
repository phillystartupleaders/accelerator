About the PSL Accelerator

What is it?

The PSL Accelerator is a 12 week program that empowers Philly's promising founders and early-stage startups to realize their vision. Each week, we guide local startups through the tough transition between business development and sales. We arm entrepreneurs with the knowledge, tools, and mentors they need to keep improving their business, while providing a safe environment where they can fail, pivot, and struggle through growing pains. Our mission is to develop talented local entrepreneurs which in turn will help to grow a robust and thriving tech community in Philadelphia.

Who is it for?

This program is a fast track for local Philadelphia startups focused on moving forward with an MVP, identifying product market fit and making their first crucial sales. This program is ideal for full time founders who plan on rooting themselves in Philadelphia and building their company here long term.

What will you get out of it?

We provide value through weekly sessions from local leaders, support from a cohort of peers and connections to additional community resources. You will be matched with strategic mentors, attend sessions led by some of Philly tech's brightest, set goals and track metrics throughout. The program will ultimately end with Demo Day where the companies will pitch to 150+ investors and community members.

FAQ

Q: How much time each week will I spend on accelerator-related activities?
You'll spend two hours on educational programming each week and one hour with your team's mentor every other week. The rest of the time is spent building your company.
Q: How many companies will be accepted into the program?
8
Q: Do I have to give up any equity?
No
Q: Will we receive any funding?
Not from the accelerator; however, you will develop strategies and tactics for pitching to investors. The program also includes a demo day where teams will pitch to local investors.
Q: Will you sign an NDA? How do I know you won't steal my idea?
Just like other accelerators and VC firms, we do not sign NDAs. We will respect the confidentiality of your venture / idea.
Q: We've been working on our startup for awhile. Is the accelerator appropriate for us?
We are happy to work with more established startups. In fact, you are required to have a product or at least an MVP that's ready to market and iterate on.
Q: Do I have to be in the Philly area?
Yes, all of the events will take place in Philly, and we don't have a way for people to participate remotely.
Q: Does our company need to be incorporated?
No, the current legal status of your startup does not matter to us.
Q: Where will the events take place?
Events will mainly take place at Benjamin's Desk Washington Square (7th & Walnut).
Q: Do I need co-founders?
Teams of two or more are more likely to succeed, and that's what we recommend for startups.
Q: What is the timeline of the program?
Applications will be accepted starting Oct 1 and ending Nov 1, interviews will take place Nov 13-30, and classes will take place from Jan 16 through Apr 3.
